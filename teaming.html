Teaming | SDVOSB

Provisions Unlimited (SDVOSB) + ProVision Systems.

Together, we combine an established Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business with focused software and integration capabilities, so you get both compliance and delivery strength on a single team.

How the partnership is structured

Provisions Unlimited is the SDVOSB prime or subcontractor of record, depending on the opportunity. ProVision Systems provides hands-on software, data, and integration delivery under that umbrella.

What Provisions Unlimited brings
  • SDVOSB status and past performance in federal contracting
  • Proposal management, compliance, and contract administration
  • Prime and sub roles depending on the acquisition strategy
What ProVision Systems brings
  • Technical leadership and architecture for app / data / AI work
  • Hands-on implementation and integration with existing systems
  • Direct collaboration with customer engineering / ops teams
If you are shaping an opportunity where an SDVOSB partner with in-house software capability would be a fit, we’re happy to talk early. Email admin@provision.systems to start the conversation.
For capture & teaming leads
Ideal opportunities
  • Lightweight applications for data collection, tracking, and reporting
  • AI-enabled workflows on top of existing document or ticket systems
  • Integration “glue” between COTS platforms and mission workflows
  • Modernization where a full rewrite is not realistic or desired
Teaming mechanics
  • We can support as a key subcontractor or under an SDVOSB prime
  • Comfortable working alongside larger integrators and OEMs
  • Happy to provide draft technical volumes and level-of-effort estimates

How to engage the team

If you have an RFI, RFP, or early-stage concept where this combined team might be a fit, send:

  • A short description of the mission / customer
  • Any draft documents (RFI, SOO, SOW, slides)
  • Rough timelines and contract vehicle if known

Email: admin@provision.systems. We’ll respond with a simple readout and recommended next steps.
