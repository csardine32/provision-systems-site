Teaming with Provisions Unlimited (SDVOSB)

ProVision Systems is the technical delivery partner for Provisions Unlimited, a Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB). Together, we offer agencies a single, small-business team that can win and deliver complex software, integration, and automation work.

Roles on a Typical Engagement

Provisions Unlimited (SDVOSB) acts as the prime:

  • Prime contractor and SDVOSB of record
  • Contract, compliance, and reporting
  • Program management and client interface

ProVision Systems delivers the technical work:

  • Solution and data architecture
  • Software development and integration
  • Automation, testing, and ongoing support

Why This Team Works

  • Meets SDVOSB set-aside and subcontracting goals.
  • Small, senior team that does the actual hands-on work.
  • Clear ownership from discovery through production support.
  • Fast path from concept or RFI to deployable solution.

What We Deliver Together

Custom Apps & Internal Portals

Secure internal web apps and portals that organize workflows, data, and approvals for operations teams, program managers, and leadership.

Built with modern stacks and designed to integrate with systems you already own.

Systems Integration & Data Pipelines

Connect line-of-business systems, security platforms, EMRs, CRMs, and cloud services so data moves automatically instead of via manual spreadsheets and email.

Results: fewer errors, better reporting, and a single source of truth.

Automation & AI-Assisted Workflows

Replace repetitive manual steps with automations and AI-assisted workflows that stay inside your security and compliance boundaries.

We focus on practical wins that reduce workload and cycle time.

NAICS & Contract Fit

Our teaming approach usually aligns with the following NAICS codes:

  • 541511 – Custom Computer Programming Services
  • 541512 – Computer Systems Design Services
  • 541513 – Computer Facilities Management Services
  • 541519 – Other Computer Related Services

We can support other codes as needed based on specific opportunity language and scope.
